Design Shanghai

Virtual but equally enjoyable. We proudly invite you to join us in the upcoming 7th edition of Design Shanghai. This time from the comfort of your home.

From 26 - 29 November, one of the world’s most prestigious international design events in Asia counts on the participation of Spain. Under the umbrella brand of Interiors from Spain, ICEX Spain Export and Investment gathers six Spanish companies and manufacturers to showcase here their latest products: furniture, textile, decorative accessories, bed linen, table linen, bath linen and ceramic tiling.

Design Shanghai aims to explore how eastern and western design philosophies can work together synergistically across the six design sections: Contemporary, Classic & Luxury, Collectibles, Kitchen & Bathroom, Workplace and New Materials & Applications.

We look forward to your visit.

ICEX / Economic and Commercial Office of the Embassy of Spain in China
